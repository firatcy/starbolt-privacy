Delete Your Starbolt Account

You can permanently delete your Starbolt account and all associated data using either of the options below.

Option 1 — Delete In-App (Recommended)

The fastest way:

  1. Open the Starbolt app
  2. Go to Settings → About & Legal → Delete Account
  3. Confirm the two-step prompt

Your account and all data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

If you can't access the app, send us an email and we'll delete your account within 30 days.

What will be deleted:

  • Your Firebase account (Apple/Google/Facebook login)
  • Your cloud save (ships, levels, progress, currency)
  • Your leaderboard entries
  • Any linked display name

What may be retained:

  • Anonymized analytics data (cannot be traced back to you)
  • Server logs older than 90 days
